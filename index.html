About ScholarPath

ScholarPath is a youth-led, mission-driven organization dedicated to dismantling the systemic barriers that prevent talented Nepali students from achieving their full potential. We operate on a core belief: talent is universal, but opportunity is not. In Nepal, a significant "information gap"—or information asymmetry—exists, where thousands of scholarships and opportunities remain inaccessible to the very students who need them most, simply because the information is scattered, hard to find, or not in their native language.

Our solution is a comprehensive ecosystem designed to bridge this gap. We combine a powerful tech platform with high-impact, on-the-ground outreach to create a sustainable pathway to opportunity for every student.

Our Vision

Our vision is a future for Nepal where every student, regardless of their geography, wealth, or social standing, has a clear and accessible path to opportunity. We envision a nation where talent is identified and nurtured from every corner, where brilliant minds from rural villages are not lost to circumstance but are empowered to become the next generation of doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders. We are building a system where information is a bridge, not a barrier, ensuring that every student's potential is seen, valued, and realized.

Our Two-Part Solution

1. The Platform

A centralized, dynamic hub that uses technology to find and match students with scholarships, internships, and other opportunities in real-time.

2. Opportunity Workshops

We "go-to-schools" to run hands-on workshops, onboarding students directly onto our platform and teaching them how to find and apply for opportunities.

3. Ambassador Clubs

We establish student-led "ScholarPath Clubs" in partner schools to create a sustainable, on-the-ground network that ensures no student is left behind.

SEE Provincial Topper Scholarships

Many provincial governments (e.g., Bagmati, Gandaki) offer scholarships for students who top the SEE exams from government schools to pursue their +2 education.

Check Provincial MOE Sites →

Ambition College Scholarships (BBA, BCA)

Ambition College in Kathmandu offers a range of scholarships for its BBA and BCA programs, including merit-based and need-based financial aid for deserving students.

View Details at ambitioncollege.edu.np →

HSEB/NEB Scholarships for +2

The National Examinations Board (NEB) provides scholarships for Class 11 and 12 in Science, Management, and other streams, often based on SEE scores and quotas.

View Details at neb.gov.np →

Dalit & Disadvantaged Student Scholarships

Specific quotas and scholarships are reserved by the Ministry of Education for students from Dalit, Janajati, Madhesi, and other disadvantaged communities.

View Details at moest.gov.np →

Himalayan Bank Foundation Scholarship

The Himalayan Bank Foundation (HBF) partners with Kathmandu University to provide scholarships for underprivileged students to pursue a BBA degree at KU.

View Details at ku.edu.np →

Budhi Ganga Hydropower Scholarship (Achham/Bajura)

This scholarship is specifically for students from the project-affected areas of Achham and Bajura districts to pursue technical fields like engineering or medicine.

Check Local Government Bulletins →

Ncell Professional Mela Scholarship

Ncell often provides scholarships for engineering and IT students at Tribhuvan University's Pulchowk Campus, based on academic merit.

View Details at ncell.axiata.com →

Karmath Scholarship (Jajarkot)

A local scholarship for meritorious but financially weak students from Jajarkot district to pursue their higher secondary education (+2) in science.

Contact Jajarkot District Education Office →

Nepal Government (Ministry of Education) Scholarships

The Government of Nepal offers a wide range of scholarships for SEE, +2, Bachelor's, and Master's students based on merit and specific quotas (e.g., Dalit, Janajati, remote areas). These are some of the most valuable opportunities available.

View Details at moest.gov.np →

UWC (United World Colleges) Nepal Scholarship

A highly prestigious full scholarship for students to complete their two-year high school (IB Diploma) at one of 18 UWC schools worldwide. Applications typically open for students aged 16-18 (Class 10-11).

View Details at np.uwc.org →

Indian Embassy Scholarships (COMPEX, etc.)

The Embassy of India in Kathmandu offers several scholarship schemes for Nepali students to pursue undergraduate courses in India, including Engineering (BE), Pharmacy (B.Pharma), and more.

View Details at indembkathmandu.gov.in →

Chinese Government Scholarships

Offered annually for Nepali students to pursue Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD degrees in various Chinese universities. These often cover tuition, accommodation, and a living stipend.

View Details at china-embassy.gov.cn →

Kathmandu University (KU) Need-Based Scholarships

KU provides financial assistance to meritorious but financially weak students for various undergraduate programs. The application process is internal after admission.

View Details at ku.edu.np →

Tribhuvan University (TU) Need-Based Scholarships

Tribhuvan University offers various scholarships and financial aid programs for students across its many constituent and affiliated campuses. These are often managed by the specific campus or institute (e.g., Institute of Engineering).

View Details (e.g., IOE) →

Private Bank & Corporate Scholarships (Nabil, Ncell)

Many private companies (like Ncell) and banks (like Nabil Bank) offer annual scholarships for students, often as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) programs for various levels.

Check Local Bank & Corporate Websites →
